The launch of the brand new 3D Sonic game Sonic Frontiers from the Sonic Team is next Tuesday, 8th November. Some of you will be waiting for the reviews to drop before making a final purchasing decision and it has been announced by Open Critic that Sonic Frontiers reviews will go live on 7th November at 7am PT / 2pm UK time. Sonic Frontiers has already leaked online and videos are being uploaded and taken down at a record pace on YouTube and other platforms, so be aware that spoilers for the new Sonic adventure are out there in the wild.
