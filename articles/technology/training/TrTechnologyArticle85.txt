Publisher Ysbryd Games and developer Panstasz announced WORLD OF HORROR is hitting full release next year.

WORLD OF HORROR is hitting full release sometime in summer 2023, alongside newly announced console releases on Nintendo Switch, PS4, and PS5. The game has been available in early access on PC (via Steam, GOG, the Microsoft Store, and Itch.io).
